Mount Slamet

Mount Slamet, Central Java's highest volcano at 3,428 meters, offers challenging treks, lush forests, and stunning summit views. It is a favorite destination for nature and adventure enthusiasts.

Mount Sumbing

Mount Sumbing, standing at 3,371 meters in Central Java, is a majestic stratovolcano known for its scenic trekking routes, lush landscapes, and panoramic views. It is a popular spot for hikers seeking adventure and natural beauty.

Mount Sindoro

Mount Sindoro is a 3,150-meter stratovolcano in Central Java, Indonesia, known for its scenic beauty, tea plantations, and challenging hikes with stunning sunrise views and a volcanic crater.
